About This Estimate

The Deurenberg formula provides a reasonable estimate for most adults:

Body Fat % = 1.20 × BMI + 0.23 × Age - 10.8 × sex - 5.4
(sex: male=1, female=0)

Understanding Body Fat Percentage

Body fat percentage is the proportion of fat mass to total body weight. Unlike BMI, which only considers weight and height, body fat percentage gives a better indication of body composition.

Healthy Body Fat Ranges

Men

  • Essential fat: 2-5%
  • Athletes: 6-13%
  • Fitness: 14-17%
  • Average: 18-24%
  • Above average: 25%+

Women

  • Essential fat: 10-13%
  • Athletes: 14-20%
  • Fitness: 21-24%
  • Average: 25-31%
  • Above average: 32%+

Limitations of This Calculator

This calculator uses the Deurenberg equation, which is based on population averages. It may not be accurate for athletes, very muscular individuals, or older adults. For a more precise measurement, consider methods like DEXA scan, hydrostatic weighing, or skinfold calipers.
